Vectors

The length of a \(n\)-dimensional vector \((c_1, ..., c_n)\) is computed by \[ |c| = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n c_i^2}\,. \] The scalar product of two \(n\)-dimensional vectors \((c_1, ..., c_n)\) and \((d_1, ..., d_n)\) is given by \[ c \cdot d = \sum_{i=1}^n c_i*d_i \,. \]
